# vision.md -- Product North Star

## Purpose
Odim is a Reality Intelligence OS that detects real corporate and state decisions before official announcements by reading capital fixation in the physical world.

## Target Users
- Tier 1: US hedge funds and quant funds.
- Tier 2: corporate strategy, M&A, and procurement teams.
- Tier 3: government and economic security agencies.
- Tier 4: PE and infrastructure funds.

## Success Criteria
- Detect reality-layer commitments with source-backed evidence and confidence.
- Connect signals through the Capital Fixation Ontology instead of showing isolated records.
- Support all eight product screens with a calm, precise, non-slop interface.
- Keep pre-commercial operating cost at zero beyond the user's existing AI subscriptions.
- Allow commercialization by replacing environment variables and source configuration, not rewriting code.

## Non-goals
- Odim is not a price prediction product.
- Odim is not a Bloomberg clone, news feed, or single-source dashboard.
- Odim does not treat Narrative Layer data as truth.
- Individual retail investors are not the initial target.

## Product Principles
- Reality over Narrative.
- Detection, not forecasting.
- Capital fixation is the highest-confidence signal.
- Every inference carries source references and confidence.
- Huginn and Munin memory is isolated by organization.

## Primary User Flows
- Analyst opens Reality Map, filters layers, and follows a signal to Entity Intelligence.
- Analyst reviews Capital Flow to compare sector commitments and Narrative-to-Reality gaps.
- Analyst uses Signal Alerts to inspect evidence chains and add entities to Watchlist.
- Analyst asks Huginn a natural-language question and receives source-backed reasoning.
- Admin reviews Audit Trail and manages team/API/settings.

## Failure Cases
- Source-free data enters the ontology.
- Narrative data is promoted to confirmed reality.
- Organization-scoped Munin memory leaks across tenants.
- AI gives deterministic-sounding answers without confidence or sources.
- UI drifts into generic AI SaaS visuals.

## Long-term Goal
Reality becomes a business decision layer that leads narrative and price.
